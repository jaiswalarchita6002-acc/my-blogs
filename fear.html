FEAR

BLOGS by ARCHITA JAISWAL – 20 September 2025

JUST A REALISATION , OR U COULD SAY "JUST A THOUGHT"

Sometimes, I fear that people in my life are just passing through. They come, stay for a while, and then leave—most often for their own convenience. Over time, I’ve noticed this isn’t just limited to relationships.

The same pattern shows up when I try to build new friendships, or even when I make professional connections. It’s as if the bond never really becomes “genuine,” no matter how much effort I put in.

I don’t necessarily see it as a flaw in myself or even as a big issue—it’s more like a recurring theme in my journey. But it does make me wonder… if true, lasting connections exist, and whether one day I’ll find someone who stays—not out of convenience, but by choice.

But anyways i don't let this fear conquer me.I do my thing and let the universe do it's miracles!.

“I’ve learned to let go of passing faces, and wait for those who stay.”
