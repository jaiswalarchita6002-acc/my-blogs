Life as an Army Kid

Growing up in an army family means a life filled with discipline, frequent moves, and unique experiences. I’ve lived in more cities than I can count and made friends from every corner of the country.

It wasn’t always easy — saying goodbye never gets fun — but it taught me to adapt, stay strong, and appreciate every moment.

I remember the pride I felt watching my parent in uniform. It shaped who I am and made me resilient.
